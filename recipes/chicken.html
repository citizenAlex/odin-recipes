Crusted Chicken Thighs

Photo by Kristóf Koródy on Unsplash

Description

This is an oldie but goodie. Shake 'N Bake without the box!

The Herbes de Provence elevates the dish a bit. You can also use Kick'n Chick'n for a bit more punch.

Ingredients

  • Package of 4 or 6 boneless, skinless, chicken thighs
  • Gluten-free bread crumbs
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Herbes de Provence

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F
  2. Rub a light coating of olive oil over the chicken thighs
  3. Generously sprinkle salt, pepper, and Herbes de Provence over both sides of the chicken
  4. Place chicken in a bag
  5. Add a generous amount of bread crumbs and seal the bag
  6. Shake
  7. Place chicken on a foil lined baking sheet
  8. Bake for 20 minutes
  9. Serve and enjoy!
