Classic Hamburgers

Photo by Lidye on Unsplash

Description

Enjoy a classic, jicy burger, perfect for a summer cookout. These simple instructions will help you mouth-watering delight for you and your family.

Yes, you too can wreate the culinary masterpiece pictured above. Knife not included.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground beef. 2 if you're hungry.
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Turn the grill on and let it get piping hot
  2. Dump the groud beef in a bowl
  3. Sprinkle, generously, the salt and pepper
  4. Splash on the sauce. Don't be shy!
  5. Mold the beef into patties. Yes with your hands! No gloves! What do you think this is McDonald's?!
  6. Slap those bad boys on the grill
  7. Stand over the hot grill to monitor things
  8. Don't press
  9. Flip 'em when you think they got the right amount of char
  10. Stand over the grill and sweat some more
  11. Take them off the grill and serve with all the accoutrements
